SOME TRIP DETAILS

We’re coming in at Long Lake, canoeing for a distance, then portaging 340m, then canoeing down buzzard to our site. You’re responsible for your own breakfast Saturday, and your own lunch Monday, we’ll handle the in between.

Just because we’re in canoes doesn’t mean you need to bring all your earthly possessions. It also feels good (for people like me) to try and be as efficient as possible.

Seriously for a moment, the difference between having fun and not having fun in rainy conditions is being dry (aka warm). Bring changes of clothes, and bring proper rain gear if you can. Pack your stuff separately in garbage bags. Being wet while camping can be horrible, but being dry is cozy/relaxing.
